London Joinery is a small company but with large project experience. We are very aware of the need for fast resolution of problems as they arise and have a clear understanding of clients' commercial imperatives. 

Our workshop manufacturing staff and London fixing team are directly employed and well-trained. Having such employees provides us with a stable workforce which in turn means that with a clear quality-control system we can ensure high standards.

We are aware of our obligations to the wider community and use only timber from sustainable sources and have a clear environmental and health & safety policy.
